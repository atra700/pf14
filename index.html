Huxhams Cross Farm

Huxhams Cross Farm lies on the edge of the Dartington Hall estate, just 10 minutes away from Totnes, South Devon. It was bought by the Biodynamic Land Trust in 2015 to be held in trust for sustainable food production in perpetuity.
